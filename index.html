✨ What's New — v1.3.0

🔔 Notification Bell
Tap 🔔 Alerts in the header to see cleaner reminders, unpaid bookings, pending reservations, and upcoming confirmed stays — all in one place.
📅 Past & Ongoing Visual States
Past empty dates are greyed out. Past bookings fade to muted colours. Ongoing bookings — stays that started in the past but haven't ended yet — pulse green so you always know what's still active.

When adding a booking that conflicts with an unpaid or pending booking, you now get a prompt to overwrite instead of a hard block.
