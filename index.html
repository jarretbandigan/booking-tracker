✨ What's New — v1.5.5

🛡️ Critical Data Protection Fix
Cancelled confirmed bookings are now archived to your booking history instead of being permanently deleted. Full cancellations and partial cancellations are both preserved with the complete original booking details.
📋 Complete Booking History
Your Guest Masterlist now shows the full history of every guest including cancelled confirmed stays. You can see exactly when a stay was cancelled and whether it was a full or partial cancellation.
💾 Backup Includes Full History
Export now captures all cancelled confirmed bookings alongside pencil history. Importing a backup restores your complete record.
